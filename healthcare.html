CARE

Your patients want to be heard. They want to feel that you genuinely care about them, their family, and their experience. Often times their issues can be very sensitive. Make them feel safe by giving them the opportunity to submit anonymous feedback via an on premise kiosk, the web, or our mobile app.

BE THERE

Don’t only collect important feedback from your patients. Show that you are listening, that you are there for them. Close feedback loops with your Patients by easily responding to feedback in real time.

BE AWARE

Your patients health is your primary concern. But you also need to monitor the overall health of your organization. Powerful data & analytics derived from feedback can help you make future decisions to improve operations and increase profitability.

  • Employees can share their experience

  • Admins see a list of pending messages to reply to

  • Employees and admins can engage in anonymous chat

  • Admins have access to analytics on their phone and desktop

How does Likemoji work?

  • After their visit, patients can share their experience via mobile app, kiosk, or web app - Anytime - Anywhere.

  • Admin team members that you predetermine (Doctors, Office Assistants, HR Directors) get push notified and can respond to the patient in real time.

  • Patients and admins can engage in private, anonymous chat to address the current experience.

  • Assigned admins have access to a dashboard packed with actionable insights, analytics and can learn from easy-to-read metrics and trends that help you keep things on the right track for you and your patients.
